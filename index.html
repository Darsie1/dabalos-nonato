Arellano School Of Computer Studies

The study of computers, including how to program them, how they operate, and how they affect society, is known as computer studies. It addresses subjects like networking, cybersecurity, hardware, software, programming languages, algorithms, and ethical issues. Students who study computers gain the ability to comprehend, design, and apply technology in a variety of contexts.
