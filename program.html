Arellano Computer Studies Degree Programs


Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BS CS)

A Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) is an undergraduate program focused on teaching students computer science fundamentals, including programming, algorithms, and software engineering, preparing them for careers in technology.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BS IT)

BSIT typically emphasizes the practical application of technology in businesses and organizations, covering areas such as network administration, database management, cybersecurity, and IT project management.

Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science (BA CS)

BACS, on the other hand, tends to have a stronger emphasis on the theoretical foundations of computer science, including algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and software development methodologies.

Specialized Programs

The Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence

program focuses on sophisticated ideas and uses of AI, such as robotics, machine learning, and natural language processing.

The Master of Science in Cybersecurity

program focuses on advanced cybersecurity subjects like digital forensics, network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking.
